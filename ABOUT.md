# HBJ (Healing by J) — Business Context

## What is HBJ?

**Healing by J (HBJ)** is a premier Korean aesthetics clinic and wellness spa based in Melbourne CBD, Australia. It has been operating for 20+ years and is regarded as one of Melbourne's most authentic Korean beauty destinations — typically fully booked weeks in advance.

The business has since expanded into three connected arms:
- **Healing by J** — the original clinic (treatments, bookings)
- **HBJ Academy** — the education arm teaching Korean beauty techniques
- **Shop Healing by J** — an online retail store selling Korean skincare products

### What They Specialise In
HBJ is best known for **authentic Korean beauty treatments** — specifically:
- **V-Shape Facial** — their signature non-surgical face contouring treatment. Uses manual massage and muscle manipulation to slim the jawline and lift facial contours. Visible results after one session.
- **LHA Chemical Peel** — a gentle lipo-hydroxy acid peel for radiance, pore refinement, and skin renewal
- **Korean Kyeong-lak (경락) Massage** — meridian-based therapy rooted in Traditional Korean Medicine. Pressure along energy pathways to improve circulation, reduce puffiness, and rejuvenate skin
- **Endermologie LPG** — clinically proven cellulite and lymphatic drainage treatment; HBJ has 20+ years of experience with this technology
- **Hydrafacial, Morpheus8, Picoway Laser** — advanced clinical equipment treatments
- **Korean Body Scrub** — traditional Italy towel (이태리 타올) scrub after a herbal bath for dramatically soft skin
- **Gung Management (궁관리)** — traditional Korean uterine health therapy including yoni herbal steam, pelvic massage
- **Cedar Bath** — described as the first and only in Melbourne
- **Cellulite Reduction / Body Contouring / RF Slimming**

### Philosophy
HBJ blends **inner health with outer beauty** — treatments address the body holistically (Korean medicine principles) rather than just surface-level cosmetics. This is a defining characteristic of their brand and curriculum.

## Korean Beauty Techniques (Key Terms for Accuracy)

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 경락 (Kyeong-lak) | Korean meridian pathways — energy channels used in Traditional Korean Medicine; applied through massage pressure rather than needles |
| V-Shape / 브이라인 | Non-surgical facial contouring targeting the jawline and facial muscles for a slimmer, lifted appearance |
| 이태리 타올 (Italy Towel) | Traditional Korean exfoliating cloth used in the body scrub ritual |
| 궁관리 (Gungwanri) | Traditional Korean uterine health therapy — yoni steam, pelvic massage, herbal tea |
| 장상 (Jangsang) | Korean body constitution assessment (similar to Ayurvedic typing) |
| 기초반 (Gicho-ban) | Foundation/beginner class |
| 고급반 (Gogeup-ban) | Advanced class |
| Endermologie | LPG mechanical device for cellulite/lymphatic drainage — HBJ has 20+ years with this |
| Morpheus8 | RF microneedling device for skin tightening and collagen remodelling |
| Picoway | Picosecond laser for pigmentation and skin rejuvenation |
